What are we about!

We are a start up business looking to start a new and revolutionary skateboard company.

We have specially made triple plywood decks of all shapes and sizes, perfect for any skater, new or old skool.

We have pro-rider designs.

So what do we have thats different I hear you ask?

Custom Designer! Yes we have a new feature that lets you design and customize your own deck, we then print the design on the deck, and ship the finished product to you. You then ride your one of a kind Demon Deck for all to see.


Want more?

We will also have a monthly competition. All designs created on Demon Decks will have the option for that design to be entered into a monthly prize draw. The winner will have there design turned into a pro-deck, and sold to millions of fans, and you get the royalties to the design. Every deck purchased with your design on it will earn you passive income for years to come.
